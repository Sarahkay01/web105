Life as we Knew it Review

Summary

The Life as we Knew it series by Susan Pfeffer is about a young girls will to survive in a changing world. A astroid pushed the moon closer to earth leaving everything off balance.

Miranda, the main character, is forced to loot for food, burn her belongings to keep warm, and sacrifice meals in order to feed her family.

The dead and The Gone

The Dead and the gone is a second book in the series. This book takes place in the city from a perspective of a little boy. While the characters all face some of the same hardships, both books show how diffrently the characters had to adapt.

This series is a easy read, and is written in a journal style format. It would be best for young adults as there is some mature language included.
